Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking – answered simply and clearly.

Do you provide a free site visit?
Yes! We offer a completely free site visit with no obligation. Our expert will visit your home, assess your requirements, take measurements and provide a detailed quotation – all at no cost.
How long does installation take?
Installation time depends on the type and quantity of work. Typically, mosquito mesh for a 2BHK takes 4–6 hours, curtains and blinds take 2–4 hours, and invisible grills are completed in 1 day. We always provide an estimated timeline during the site visit.
Do you provide customized sizes?
Absolutely! All our products are custom-made to fit your exact window, door or balcony dimensions. Our technicians take precise measurements to ensure a perfect fit every time.
What areas of Hyderabad do you serve?
We serve all areas of Hyderabad including Madinaguda, Kondapur, Gachibowli, Kukatpally, Miyapur, HITEC City, Madhapur, Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Ameerpet, and surrounding areas.
Do you provide a warranty on your products?
Yes, we provide warranty on all our installations. PHIFER mosquito mesh comes with manufacturer warranty. Invisible grills and other structural products come with 1–3 year warranty depending on the product. Warranty details are shared in writing after installation.
How can I get a quotation?
Getting a quote is easy! Call us at 9666112814, WhatsApp us, or fill our contact form and we'll arrange a free site visit. After measurement, you'll receive a detailed quote within 24 hours.
